First launch: right-click → Open (don't double-click). HIVE isn't yet signed with an Apple Developer certificate, so macOS Gatekeeper will block a normal double-click on first launch. Getting started
1
Download the .dmg for your Mac
Choose Apple Silicon (M-series) or Intel above. Not sure? Check Apple menu → About This Mac.
2
Mount and drag to Applications
Open the .dmg file, then drag HIVE into your Applications folder as prompted.
3
Right-click → Open on first launch
Because HIVE is unsigned, Gatekeeper will block a double-click. Right-click → Open the first time only.
4
Make sure OpenCode CLI is installed
HIVE manages OpenCode sessions — you'll need opencode installed and on your PATH. After that, HIVE will pick it up automatically.
